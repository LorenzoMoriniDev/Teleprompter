A Simple, Free & Professional Web Teleprompter

This tool is designed to help you speak fluently and confidently on camera, during a public speech, or for a presentation. Turn your browser into a professional speech prompter with a single click. Just paste your script, adjust the scroll speed and font size, and you're ready to go.

How Does Our Web Teleprompter Work?

Using our prompter tool is incredibly simple and requires no sign-up or installation:

  • Paste Your Text: Copy and paste your speech into the "Your Script" area.
  • Customize: Set the words per minute (WPM), font size, and activate mirror mode if needed.
  • Start Prompting: Click the Start button to begin the smooth, constant-speed text scroll.

It's the perfect tool for content creators, YouTubers, students, and professionals looking for a reliable and free teleprompter solution.
